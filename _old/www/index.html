A little bit about me

I've been working with IT since 1997 when I started in a support role at Deloitte. Over 10 years at Deloitte I was involved in lots of exciting and interesting projects and it was during this time I moved into software development and became a developer. By the time I left Deloitte in 2007 I had over 6 years experience developing application with the Microsoft Stack.

After leaving Deloitte in 2007 I decided to enter the world of contracting and in 2008 had my first experience with Microsoft Dynamics CRM. Since then I have stayed faithful to MS CRM. To date I have completed project to implement MS CRM in many different scenarios. Integrating MS CRM with a wide range of systems from AS400 to Web API's to Master Data Management. I've also been involved in many migrations and upgrades and have experience and knowledge of tools such as Scribe and SSIS.

Outside of work I have a wonderful wife and two beautiful children, who do their level best to keep me busy! I play rugby and coach mini and junior rugby with the remainder of my spare time.
